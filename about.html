About Spondle

Spondle is an event discovery and management platform designed to make it easier for organisers to create, share, and promote events of all kinds — from live music and comedy to community gatherings and festivals.

Our goal is to connect event seekers with organisers in a way that’s seamless, engaging, and visually appealing. Whether you’re managing a small local gig or a full tour, Spondle gives you the tools to showcase your events beautifully.

Event organisers can create custom microsites, integrate their events on their own websites using our plugin, and track engagement through analytics dashboards — all powered by a modern, user-friendly interface.

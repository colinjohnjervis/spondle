Embeddable Widget (Preview)

Drop a tiny snippet on your website to display your Spondle events automatically. Style it to match your brand.

<!-- Spondle Events Widget -->
<div id="spondle-widget" data-organiser="YOUR_ORG_ID"></div>
<script src="https://cdn.spondle.com/widget.min.js" async></script>

This is a placeholder API and script URL for now — we’ll wire it up in the prototype phase.

What it will do

  • Auto-pulls your events from Spondle
  • Filters by dates, categories, tags
  • Matches your site’s fonts & colours
  • No coding needed
